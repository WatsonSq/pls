About Progress Labs

We believe progress is nonpartisan.

"The left and the right argue over who drives. We're here to fix the road."
— Progress Labs Manifesto

Progress Labs is an independent campaign studio. We exist to identify policies and systems that are demonstrably holding people back — regardless of which political tribe supports or opposes them — and build the public pressure needed to change them.

We don't endorse parties or candidates. We don't have a left-wing or right-wing agenda. We have one agenda: things that work. Our campaigns are grounded in evidence, designed for reach, and built to win.

From student debt to housing, healthcare to infrastructure, we find the places where political gridlock is costing ordinary people the most — and we act.

  • 01Evidence first. Every campaign starts with rigorous research. If the data doesn't support it, we don't run it.
  • 02No tribalism. We work with whoever is right on the issue, full stop. Political identity is irrelevant.
  • 03Measurable impact. We define success before we start and report on it honestly when we finish.
  • 04Open by default. Our research, our methods, and our results are public. Scrutinise us.

How We Work

A rigorous, repeatable playbook for change.

Step 01

Identify the problem

We look for systems where the evidence of harm is clear and bipartisan action is theoretically possible but politically blocked. High-leverage, high-neglect.

Step 02

Build the case

Rigorous research, accessible writing, and honest numbers. We make the evidence undeniable — for policymakers, journalists, and the public alike.

Step 03

Mobilise and campaign

We build coalitions across the political spectrum, design campaigns for organic reach, and sustain pressure until something changes. Then we measure whether it did.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Who we are

Progress Labs is an independent, non-partisan campaign studio. This policy covers the website at progresslabs.co.uk and any campaign microsites we operate, including abolishstudentloans.co.uk and fixplanning.uk.

What data we collect

When you submit our contact form we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry and, if you consent, to keep you updated on campaigns relevant to your interests.

We do not collect sensitive personal data and we do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies & analytics

We may use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how people find and use our site (e.g. page views, referral sources). We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. If we use third-party analytics, we configure them to anonymise IP addresses and disable cross-site tracking.

How we store your data

Any personal data you submit is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data against unauthorised access.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also object to processing or request data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@progresslabs.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

Third-party links

Our site links to external campaign websites and resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above will reflect any changes. Continued use of our sites after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
